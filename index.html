How to Use

  • Enter a number and click "Insert" to add it to the BST
  • Enter a number and click "Delete" to remove it from the BST
  • Enter a number and click "Search" to find it in the BST
  • Click on any traversal button to see the traversal order
  • Use "Reset Tree" to start over with an empty tree
